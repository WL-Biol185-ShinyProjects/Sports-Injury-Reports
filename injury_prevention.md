## General Tips

- Always warm up before activity and cool down afterward
- Stay hydrated before, during, and after exercise
- Know your limits and gradually increase intensity
- Get adequate rest and recovery between sessions

## Equipment

- Wear appropriate protective gear for your sport
- Make sure equipment fits properly
- Replace worn out shoes and gear regularly

## Technique

- Learn proper form from a coach or trainer
- Avoid overuse by varying your activities
- Listen to your body and stop if something hurt
